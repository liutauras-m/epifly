01 / Identity

A brand that feels like a breakthrough in motion.

The Epifly system combines a vivid directional mark, disciplined geometric typography, and a minimal interface palette that keeps every asset legible and energetic. The brand never feels chaotic; speed is conveyed through contrast, rhythm, and confident restraint.

Core idea

Epiphany, not overload

Every expression should suggest insight arriving faster than expected, without clutter or decorative excess.

Mark behavior

Sharp, ascending, untwisted

The swallow-starburst form should remain crisp and directional. It can glow, invert, or recolor, but it should never distort.

Product feel

Premium speed

Rounded interfaces, clean spacing, and controlled motion create a calm surface with energetic response underneath.

02 / Palette

Signal colors built for clarity and contrast.

The palette centers on a warm orange for action and recognition, with electric cyan reserved for highlights, hover energy, and product intelligence. Deep charcoal and warm off-white maintain premium legibility across light and dark surfaces.

03 / Typography

Geist keeps the system modern, precise, and crisp.

Use Geist Sans for wordmark, UI, and body content. Geist Mono supports code, labels, and meta cues. Headings should feel compact and intentional with tight tracking, while body copy remains open and readable.
